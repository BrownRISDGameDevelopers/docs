We're always looking to welcome new members, regardless of skill level, into our club!

If you are interested in joining, feel free to show up at any our weekly meetings and speak with our E-Board. We'd be happy to show you the ropes and have you contribute your skills on any project!

Join our [Discord](https://discord.gg/dhrKyse6RH) to find out when our weekly meetings are, or alternatively, subscribe to our [listserv](https://lists.cs.brown.edu/wws/subscribe/gamedev?previous_action=info) (requires a `brown.edu` or `risd.edu` email).
